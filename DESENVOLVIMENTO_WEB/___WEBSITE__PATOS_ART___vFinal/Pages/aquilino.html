Adaptation of Aquilino Ribeiro's book "Cinco Réis de Gente" as a graphic novel of 70 pages, adding up to 800 drawings.
Published in May 2023 in the 5th edition of "aquilino", the literary magazine by the Sernancelhe County, the homeland of the writer. Aquilino Ribeiro (1885-1963) was a notable, very sagacious portuguese writer, brave both in his literary legacy, as well as in the way he leaded his life.
