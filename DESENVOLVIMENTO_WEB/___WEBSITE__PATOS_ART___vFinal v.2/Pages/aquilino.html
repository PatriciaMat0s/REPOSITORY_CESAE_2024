Adaptation of Aquilino Ribeiro's book "Cinco Réis de Gente" as a graphic novel, published in May 2023, in the 5th edition of "aquilino", the literary magazine by the Sernancelhe County, the homeland of the writer. Aquilino Ribeiro (1885-1963) was a notable, very sagacious portuguese writer, brave both in his literary legacy but also in the way he leaded his own life.
