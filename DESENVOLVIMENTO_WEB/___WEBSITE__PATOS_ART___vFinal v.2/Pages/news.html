The Magazine "aquilino" 5th ed. is completed

An edition that i fully illustrated as a graphic novel. Aquilino Ribeiro (1885-1963) was an illustrious and very sagacious portuguese writer. His memory is well kept alive by this magazine, a publication by the writer's hometown County.
