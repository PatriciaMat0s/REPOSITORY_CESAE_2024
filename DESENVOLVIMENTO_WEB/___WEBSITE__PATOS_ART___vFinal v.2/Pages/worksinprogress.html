Starting point is someone, usually a selfportrait, from memory.
Figurative, sure; however, the focus on the form itself, the outer shell (ie, being solely a good tecnician) was never the aim. Quite the opposite!
One just needs a basis to be deconstructed, scribbled on, scratched on, experimented on, failed, partially erased, abandoned, forgotten - then resumed, tried again, redone.
Searching for some honesty and crudeness, while denying superfluous refinenements that detract from the essencial and the truth about something. This process tries to go hand in hand with life - that is, the act of trying to live. Trying to make sense of all this. Crude yet elusive, rigid yet delicate, self-assured yet evanescent feeling...
